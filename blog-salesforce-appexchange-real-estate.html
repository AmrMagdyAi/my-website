Best Salesforce Apps for Real Estate: AppExchange Guide 2025

Amr Magdy
February 2025 · 6 min read Salesforce CRM Consultant

One of Salesforce's biggest advantages for real estate companies is the AppExchange — a marketplace of over 5,000 pre-built integrations and applications that extend Salesforce's capabilities without custom development. Here are the apps most relevant to real estate teams in 2025.

E-Signature: DocuSign for Salesforce

The most-used AppExchange app for real estate. DocuSign integrates directly with Salesforce Opportunities and Contacts, allowing agents to send, track, and store signed contracts without leaving Salesforce. When a document is signed, the status automatically updates in the related Opportunity record and triggers any configured follow-up workflows.

Why it matters: Signed contracts often trigger the next phase of a transaction — document execution to due diligence, for example. With DocuSign connected to Salesforce, this stage transition happens automatically.

Transaction Management: SkySlope for Salesforce

SkySlope is one of the leading transaction management platforms for real estate brokerages, and its Salesforce integration syncs transaction status, compliance checklists, and document completion back to Salesforce Opportunities. Brokers see compliance status without leaving their CRM.

Telephony: Salesforce CTI Integration

Several CTI (Computer Telephony Integration) apps connect your phone system to Salesforce: calls are logged automatically, contacts are screen-popped when a known number calls, and call recordings are attached to the relevant Contact or Lead record. RingCentral for Salesforce and Kixie are both strong options used by real estate teams.

For real estate companies doing significant outbound calling — investor acquisitions, seller outreach — a CTI integration eliminates all manual call logging and gives managers full visibility into call activity without asking agents to self-report.

Marketing Automation: Pardot / Marketing Cloud Account Engagement

Salesforce's own marketing automation platform, now called Marketing Cloud Account Engagement, integrates natively with the CRM. For real estate: automated email nurture sequences for long-term buyer prospects, seller education sequences, past client re-engagement campaigns, and detailed engagement tracking that surfaces hot leads to agents.

Data Enrichment: Clearbit for Salesforce

Automatically enriches Contact and Lead records with company information, social profiles, and firmographic data. For real estate companies targeting specific buyer personas (tech executives, relocating families, investors in specific asset classes), enrichment data helps agents personalize outreach without manual research.

Reporting: Salesforce CRM Analytics (Einstein Analytics)

The native Salesforce analytics platform goes beyond standard reports and dashboards. For real estate leadership, it enables: cross-object analytics (connecting lead source data to closed deal data across time), AI-powered forecasting, and mobile-optimized executive dashboards. It's a step up from Tableau for teams that want everything within Salesforce.

How to Evaluate AppExchange Apps

  • Check the reviews — filter for reviews from real estate companies specifically
  • Verify compatibility — confirm the app supports your Salesforce edition (Professional vs Enterprise)
  • Test in sandbox first — install in a sandbox environment before production
  • Check support quality — a powerful app with poor support is a liability in a production environment
